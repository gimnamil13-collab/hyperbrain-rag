# Weekly Review Checklist

Use this every Sunday (or any fixed day) to keep your Second Brain useful.

## 1. Clear Inbox (10 min)

- Move new notes into PARA folders
- Delete junk captures
- Tag or link related notes

## 2. Review Active Projects (15 min)

- What is blocked?
- What is the next action?
- Archive finished items

## 3. Feed the RAG system (5 min)

- Export 1-2 new notes as `.md` or `.pdf`
- Upload to your knowledge base
- Ask one test question to verify retrieval

## 4. Reflect (5 min)

- What did I learn this week?
- What should I read or capture next week?

## Why this matters for RAG

A RAG system is only as good as the documents you index.
Stale or messy notes → weak answers.
A short weekly review keeps retrieval quality high.
